Publications & Presentations

 

  • Art has written or been a contributor to several articles related to farm business planning for popular farm publications such as Country Guide and Grainews 

    • Several years ago he co-authored a series of articles on farm succession for Grainews.

    • In the February 12, 2019 edition of Grainews he wrote a column entitled “Five reasons to make a farm business plan.”

    • In the fall of 2019 he wrote three articles for Grainews on “Facing and addressing farm financial issues” which appeared in the September 24, 2019, October 15, 2019 and November 5, 2019 issues. You can request copies of these via the contact form.

    • He was the contributor to an article in the February 2020 edition of Canadian Cattlemen entitled “Don’t fall into a ditch when expanding your land base”.

  • Presenting seminars and workshops

    • Art has worked with other professionals such as accountants and lawyers to present seminars on succession planning.

    • Most recently he presented a talk at Northlands Farmfair on “Farm business planning and addressing financial challenges. 

    • For the 2020 Alberta Young Farmers & Ranchers annual general meeting Art gave a presentation entitled Farm Business Planning – the Big Picture.

  • Art is available to present workshops and seminars on any topic related to farm business or succession planning alone or with other presenters. Contact Art.
